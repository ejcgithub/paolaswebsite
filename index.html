OUR PROGRAM

STRONG & HEALTHY FROM INSIDE OUT is a nutritional and workout program, serving men and woman who want to gain knowledge about their personal nutritional needs. Through comprehensive and individualized counselling sessions, each client learns the amazing tool to count their Macro Nutrients, which is unique and personalized to each of them. Paired with our workout program, you will be in the healthiest and fittest in your life.

Weight Lifting

Comprise using weights to build muscles, this program will help you metabolise those pesky stored fats

Callisthenics

Using your body as weights and a limited amount of space, you can do callisthenics anywhere in your home or the world!

Cardio HIIT

Fast pace cardiovascular workout that will take your heart rate sky high then paired with the same amount of rest.

Nutrition

For us to function at 100%, we will educate you with the right amount of nutrition while eating a balanced, delicious meal.

About Our Company

Our company was born in an unprecedented time in March 2020. The world was under a lockdown and Paola acknowledged that her friends, family and community needed a place to stay in shape and be a part of a group.Stronger and Healthy started as a virtual base work out a program that grew into summer bootcamp and nutritional consulting team. We have over 100 members both locally and online.
